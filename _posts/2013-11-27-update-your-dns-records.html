I mentioned recently our plans to move to new hosting servers. Aside from those of you who asked for their accounts to be moved on a specific data, this is now all complete.

If a domain name of yours is used with Hosting Reborn and you don't use the Hosting Reborn nameservers, you must update your DNS records.

If you have any A records pointing to 178.63.85.207, these must be changed to 144.76.222.164.
