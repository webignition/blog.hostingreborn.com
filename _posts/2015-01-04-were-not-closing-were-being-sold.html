We're no longer closing down and instead are selling to a competent and capable company who will continue operating the service.

We're not closing down, we're selling

Following our initial closing down announcement, we were contacted by various parties interested in purchasing the business.

I had previously considered selling the service as an alternative to closing down, however I wasn't certain how easily I could find a buyer who would be capable of operating the service competently and in the same spirit.

We were recently contacted by Purple Tiger Media Ltd with a proposal to purchase Hosting Reborn.

I'm convinced that they have the technical capability to continue operating the service smoothly.

We're yet to agree on the details of the sale, however I'm certain that the service will be able to continue to operate.
